Unfiltered Movie Review, According to David O

These are my thoughts on 3 movies I've seen so far in 2024

Saltburn

This one was a ride. Grave stuff, tub stuff, party stuff - so many twists and turns that made me cringe.
Rating: 8.5/10

Promising Young Woman

Don't get me wrong, the premise was cool. And I was interested by the plot. But the acting was well below subpar and the movie became somewhat predictable.
Rating: 5.9/10

Killers of the Flower Moon

Leo does it again. Did it really have to be over 3 hours? I'm not sure. But did Leo cook??? 1000%
Rating: 8.3/10
